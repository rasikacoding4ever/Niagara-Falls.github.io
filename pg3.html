About the Creator


Rasika Sivakumar is a rising 8th grader at Arcola Intermediate School in Philly, PA. She has various interests such as music, dance, violin, drawing, tennis, and of course, coding. She enjoys reading, gardening, and staying active in her free time. Rasika has won in several science fairs and international music competitions. She also has been performing vocal concerts worldwide since the age of 8. For the past 2 years, Rasika has been performing concerts virtually on online platforms due to the Coronavirus pandemic. One of her future aspirations is to attend Harvard University and work in the medical field as a surgical Oncologist.
